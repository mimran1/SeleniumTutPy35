Faculty & Advisor Records Access

  

Reminder: Users may access records to support University business only, in accord with our Computer and Network Usage Policy (Acceptable Use Policy). All users accepted these policies when first logging in.



  Term Selection
Set the term (semester and year) you would like to review.
  ID Selection
Select a student ID to begin interacting with a record.
 

Student Academic Records
  Student Information
Access summary academic information for selected students. Includes: major, program, status, residency, standing, class.
  Academic Transcripts
Access unofficial online versions of the complete official transcript. This tool features much of the former GP20 data, including by-semester summaries and GPA calculations.
  Banner DARS
Order DARS audits or review existing DARS audits for selected students.
  Advanced Placement & Transfer Summary (SR44)
View detailed transfer records and AP scores for selected students.
  Student Schedule
Access schedules for selected students.
  Active Registrations (Summary Schedule)
View a registration summary for a selected student.

  Registration History
View online registration transactions for a selected student.
 

Student Biographic and Demographic Information
  Student Address and Phones
Review address and telephone information for your students and advisees.
  Student E-mail Addresses
View current official email addresses for students and advisees.
  View Student Holds
View holds that may restrict registration for students.
 

Course Records
  View Class Rosters
Enter a CRN to view class lists for a selected course.
  Complete Schedule of Classes
View the grid view schedule of classes.
 

Specialized Tools
  Specialized Faculty and Staff Tools
Access Binghamton University developed tools for Faculty and Staff. Most of these tools are developed for specific programs, and require access grants.
